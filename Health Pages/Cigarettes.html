Health Information: Cigarettes

One cigarette, on average, takes 6 minutes to smoke and subtracts 11 minutes from your life.

The average American smoker smokes 19 cigarettes per day and starts smoking when they are 17 years old.


Assuming an original life expectancy of 80 years, smoking 19 cigarettes per day, and starting to smoke when 17 years old, a smoker loses 9.144 years of their life, for a new life expectancy of 70.86 years.

Sources:

Mortality in relation to smoking: 40 years' observations on male British doctors
